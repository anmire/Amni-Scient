AMNI-AI — PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 3, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 3, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Amni-AI, a local intelligence engine developed by Amni-Scient ("we", "us", "our"), handles user information.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect no data. Amni-AI does not require account creation, registration, or any form of authentication. The application runs entirely on your local machine with no network dependency for core operation.

2. DATA HANDLING

All model inference, knowledge storage, self-improvement cycles, and model operations execute on your hardware using your GPU and local storage. No queries, responses, learning data, or model state are transmitted externally. The Dictionary Atlas and all associated texture data reside on your local disk and VRAM exclusively. Self-improvement deltas are stored in a local sandbox on your machine.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Amni-AI does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, telemetry, or cloud services. The application operates entirely offline once installed. No API keys are required for core operation.

4. DATA SHARING

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. No information leaves your machine.

5. DATA RETENTION

All application data exists only on your local machine under your control. Uninstalling Amni-AI removes all associated data. Knowledge bases, model files, and learning sandboxes are stored in the application's local directory.

6. SECURITY

All model inference, knowledge retrieval, and self-improvement operations execute on local hardware. The Dictionary Atlas resides on local storage and VRAM. Internal integrity is enforced via SHA-256 hash seals on protected system files, ensuring core safety axioms cannot be modified by the self-improvement subsystem. No data is transmitted externally.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Amni-AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, no special provisions apply.

8. PERMISSIONS

Amni-AI runs as a standard desktop application with user-level permissions. No elevated or administrative privileges are required. GPU access is used for computational acceleration (Vulkan/DirectML) only.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the application. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.
