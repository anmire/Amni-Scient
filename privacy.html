PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 15, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 15, 2025

This privacy policy describes how Amni-Crypt ("the App"), developed and published by Amni-Scient ("we", "us", "our"), handles user information. This policy applies to the Amni-Crypt Android application distributed via Google Play Store.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect no personal data. Amni-Crypt does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information. No accounts are required. No registration, login, email address, phone number, name, or location is ever requested or recorded.

All encryption and decryption operations are performed entirely on your device. No files, passphrases, encryption keys, cipher seals, QR codes, or any user content ever leaves your device via our app.

2. FILES AND STORAGE

Amni-Crypt uses Android's scoped storage model. The app accesses only files you explicitly select through the system file picker. Encrypted output files (.acrypt) are saved to your device's Downloads directory. The app does not scan, index, or access any files beyond those you directly provide for encryption or decryption.

3. CAMERA

Amni-Crypt includes a QR code scanner that uses your device camera (via CameraX + ML Kit). The camera is activated only when you explicitly open the scanner. No photos or video are captured, stored, or transmitted. Camera frames are processed in real-time on-device to detect QR codes and are immediately discarded.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

4.1 Google AdMob

The free version of Amni-Crypt displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google's AdMob SDK may collect and process the following data as described in Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (for ad serving geolocation; not shared with us)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • App usage data related to ad display

We do not have access to this data. It is collected and processed entirely by Google under their privacy policy. Premium subscribers who remove ads will not have any AdMob data collection active.

4.2 Google Play Billing

Premium subscription purchases are handled entirely by Google Play's billing system. Payment information (credit card, payment method) is managed by Google and is never visible to or collected by us. We receive only a purchase token confirming subscription status.

4.3 Google ML Kit

The QR scanner uses Google ML Kit for barcode detection. ML Kit runs on-device and does not transmit camera data or scan results to Google servers. See ML Kit Terms.

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, trade, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. The only third-party data processing occurs through Google AdMob as described in Section 4.1, and is governed by Google's privacy policy.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain no user data because we collect no user data. Encrypted files exist only on your device under your control. Uninstalling the app removes all app-related data from your device (disclaimer acceptance preference, etc.).

7. SECURITY

Amni-Crypt employs a proprietary 1024-bit block cipher with 16 rounds of substitution-permutation transformation, PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 key derivation (600,000 iterations), and HMAC-SHA256 integrity verification. All cryptographic operations execute locally. No encryption keys or passphrases are stored by the app after an operation completes.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Amni-Crypt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app's content rating is Teen due to cryptographic subject matter.

9. PERMISSIONS

  • CAMERA — Required for QR code scanner. Used only when scanner is explicitly opened.
  • INTERNET — Required for AdMob ad serving and Google Play Billing verification. No user data is transmitted.
  • READ/WRITE STORAGE — Scoped storage access for reading files you select and writing encrypted output to Downloads.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Since we collect no personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, export, or delete. If you wish to stop AdMob data collection, you may: (a) subscribe to premium to disable ads, (b) reset your Android Advertising ID, or (c) opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
